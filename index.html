01 · The Problem

SMALL FARMERS,
GIANT CHALLENGES

Millions of smallholder farmers worldwide face unpredictable weather, inefficient water use, and a complete lack of real-time field intelligence. Traditional methods can’t keep pace with accelerating climate variability — leaving food systems vulnerable and yields in freefall.

▲ 40% crop loss from climate events ▼ 70% lack precision data Irregular rainfall patterns Soil degradation No satellite access
02 · Our Solution

SATELLITE DATA
MEETS THE FIELD

Agronimbus bridges the gap between NASA’s powerful Earth observation satellites and the farmer’s daily decisions. By fusing multi-source remote sensing with AI-driven analytics, we translate raw space data into simple, actionable recommendations.

Real-Time Satellite Monitoring
Live NDVI, soil moisture, and land surface temperature feeds from NASA’s MODIS, Landsat, and Sentinel satellites updated every 24 hours.
AI Crop Analytics
Machine learning models trained on historical agricultural data provide early warning for pest outbreaks, drought stress, and optimal harvest windows.
Smart Irrigation Advice
Precision recommendations that reduce water usage by up to 35% while maintaining or improving yield quality — tailored per parcel and crop type.
Yield Prediction
Season-ahead forecasts based on climate models, satellite vegetation indices, and region-specific crop calendars to plan harvests and market timing.
